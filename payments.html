Payments

Payment for goods is made by bank card through the authorization server of the Bank's Processing Center. Please have your plastic card ready in advance.

1. Payment Methods

— Visa;
— Mastercard;
— American Express;
— and more.

2. Data Transfer

Please have your plastic card ready in advance. Connection with the payment gateway and transmission of information is performed in secure mode using the SSL encryption protocol.

If your bank supports Verified By Visa or MasterCard Secure Code secure online payment technology, you may also be required to enter a special password to make a payment. Check with your card issuing bank to find out how and if you can get passwords for online payments.

This site supports 128-bit encryption. Our partners ensure the confidentiality of the personal information they share. The entered information will not be given to the third parties except the cases provided by the legislation. Making payments by bank cards is carried out in strict accordance with the requirements of payment systems Visa Int. and MasterCard Europe Sprl.

3. Payment Process

If you choose the method of payment by plastic card, the payment for the order is made immediately after its execution. After completing the order you will have to click on the "Pay" button, the system will switch you to the page of the authorization server, where you will be prompted to enter the plastic card data and initiate its authorization by clicking "Pay with bank card". In case of authorization you will be redirected to the thank you page. If the authorization of the card is rejected, you will be able to repeat the payment procedure. After the payment has been made, you will receive a confirmation SMS (optional) on your cell phone.

To pay by card, you need to enter the following data:

— Card number, shown on the front of the card, usually 16 digits;
— CVV2/CVC2 code, is on the back of the card - these are the last 3 digits that are separated from the others;
— The name and surname of the cardholder, it is necessary to specify strictly in Latin letters;
— The expiration date is on the front of your card, just below the card number.

4. Successful Authorization

After successful payment, you will be taken to the thank you page. An email with order details and tracking number will be sent to your email address.

5. Payment Rejection

— Bank card data was entered incorrectly;
— There are not enough funds on your bank card. You can find out more about the availability of funds by contacting the bank that issued your bank card;
— The expiration date of the bank card has expired. The expiration date of the card is usually indicated on the front of the card (it is the month and year until which the card is valid). You can find out more about the card expiration date by contacting your bank;
— The bank card is not designed to make payments over the Internet, as you can find out by contacting the bank that issued the bank card.

The personal information you provide (name, address, phone number, e-mail, bank card number) is confidential and will not be disclosed. Your credit card data is transmitted only in encrypted form and is not stored on the bank's web server.
